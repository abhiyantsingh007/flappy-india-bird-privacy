1. Introduction

Flappy India Bird ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the choices you have when using our app. By using Flappy India Bird, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Scope & Target Audience

This app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location. The app may collect the following non-personal information:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, screen size, and language (used for compatibility and diagnostics).
  • Gameplay and usage data: session length, scores, level progress, and aggregated usage trends used to improve gameplay and balance.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics: anonymized error logs to help diagnose and fix issues.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information to improve game performance and stability, fix bugs and crashes, and understand user behavior to guide product improvements. We do not use the information to create individually identifiable user profiles or to contact users.

We do not sell personal information. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable analytics or crash reports with trusted service providers who assist with analytics and app stability. Any third-party service we use will only receive the data necessary to provide their service and will be contractually bound to protect that data.

If we add analytics or advertising SDKs in the future, we will disclose their names here and update the Play Console Data Safety form accordingly.

If we add advertising later, we will update this policy and the Data Safety information in the Play Console. If ads are shown to children, we will only use ad providers certified for family-safe ads and will disable personalized advertising for users identified as children.

7. Data Retention

We retain non-personal analytics and crash data only as long as needed to support app improvements and troubleshooting—typically no longer than 24 months—unless otherwise required for legal or safety reasons.
